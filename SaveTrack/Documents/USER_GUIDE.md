## Adding Entries

Logging your savings is quick and easy!

### Quick Add Entry

1. **Tap the "+" button** (floating action button) on the Home screen
   - Or tap "Add Entry" in the Home screen

![Add Entry Button](screenshots/add-entry-button.png)

*Screenshot: Floating action button with + icon*

2. **Fill in the entry form:**

![Entry Form](screenshots/entry-form.png)

*Screenshot: Entry form showing amount field, category selection grid, note field, and save button*

#### Amount
- Enter the amount you saved
- Supports decimal values (e.g., $5.50)
- Currency symbol based on your settings

#### Category
- Select from predefined categories:
  - üõí **Skipped Purchase** - Didn't buy something you wanted
  - üéüÔ∏è **Used Coupon** - Saved with a coupon or discount
  - üè† **Cooked at Home** - Saved by cooking instead of eating out
  - üö´ **Canceled Subscription** - Canceled a recurring service
  - üí° **Cheaper Option** - Chose a less expensive alternative
  - ‚ûï **Other** - Any other savings
- Categories are displayed as buttons with emoji icons
- Tap to select (selected category is highlighted)

#### Note (Optional)
- Add a brief description
- Examples: "Skipped coffee shop", "Used 20% off coupon", "Made pasta at home"
- Helps you remember what you saved on

#### Save as Template (Optional)
- Check the box if you want to save this entry as a template
- Templates let you quickly add similar entries later
- You'll be prompted to name the template

3. **Tap "Save Entry"**
   - Entry is saved immediately
   - You'll return to the Home screen
   - Your streak and today's total update automatically

### Using Templates

If you've saved entry templates:

1. Tap "Use Template" button in the entry form
2. Select a template from the list
3. The form is pre-filled with template data
4. Adjust amount or note if needed
5. Tap "Save Entry"

![Entry Templates](screenshots/entry-templates.png)

*Screenshot: List of saved entry templates with category, amount, and note preview*

You can log multiple entries in a single day! Each entry counts toward:
- Your daily total

- Your goal progress

**Tip:** Log entries throughout the day as you make saving decisions!

## Tips & Best Practices

### Building a Streak

1. **Log entries daily** - Even small amounts count!
2. **Set a reminder** - Use the daily reminder notification
3. **Log immediately** - Don't wait until the end of the day
4. **Multiple entries are fine** - Log throughout the day as you save

### Setting Effective Goals

1. **Start small** - Set achievable initial goals
2. **Be specific** - Name goals clearly (e.g., "Vacation Fund" not "Save Money")
3. **Set deadlines** - Deadlines create urgency
4. **Review regularly** - Check progress weekly

### Using Categories

1. **Use specific categories** - Helps identify spending patterns
2. **Create custom categories** - For recurring savings types
3. **Add notes** - Notes help you remember context later

### Maximizing Insights

1. **Log consistently** - More data = better insights
2. **Review charts weekly** - Spot trends and patterns
3. **Check insights regularly** - Get personalized recommendations

### Data Backup

1. **Export monthly** - Regular backups protect your data
2. **Save to iCloud Drive** - Automatic cloud backup
3. **Export before major updates** - Safety first!

## Troubleshooting

### Common Issues

#### Streak Not Updating

**Problem:** Streak shows 0 days even though you've logged entries.

**Solutions:**
- Make sure you're logging at least one entry per day
- Check that entries are being saved (view History)
- Streak requires consecutive days - missing a day resets it
- Close and reopen the app to refresh

#### Goals Not Showing Progress

**Problem:** Goal progress bar doesn't update after adding entries.

**Solutions:**
- Ensure entries are within the goal's date range
- Check that the goal period (monthly/yearly) matches your entries
- Goals created later will include past entries from the start date
- Try refreshing by closing and reopening the app

#### Categories Not Appearing

**Problem:** Categories don't show in the entry form.

**Solutions:**
- Close and reopen the app
- Check Settings ‚Üí Categories to verify categories exist
- If still missing, delete and reinstall the app (export data first!)

#### Currency Not Changing

**Problem:** Currency setting doesn't update amounts.

**Solutions:**
- Close and reopen the app
- Check Settings ‚Üí Currency is set correctly
- All amounts should update automatically

#### App Crashes

**Problem:** App crashes when opening or using features.

**Solutions:**
- Force close the app and reopen
- Restart your iPhone
- Check that you're running iOS 15.0 or later
- If persistent, export your data and reinstall the app

#### Data Not Saving

**Problem:** Entries disappear after closing the app.

**Solutions:**
- Make sure you tap "Save Entry" (don't just close the form)
- Check that you have sufficient storage space
- Verify the app has proper permissions
- Export data as backup before troubleshooting

### Getting Help

If you encounter issues not covered here:

1. **Check the console** (if using Xcode) for error messages
2. **Export your data** as a backup
3. **Restart the app** and try again
4. **Review this guide** for feature-specific help

## Frequently Asked Questions

### Can I use SaveTrack on multiple devices?

Currently, SaveTrack stores data locally on each device. To sync between devices:
1. Export data from Device 1
2. Import data on Device 2
3. Repeat as needed

**Note:** This creates separate copies, not true sync. Future versions may include optional cloud sync.

### What happens if I delete the app?

All data is deleted when you uninstall the app. **Always export your data before deleting!**

### How long are streaks tracked?

Streaks are tracked indefinitely. Your longest streak is saved permanently. Current streaks reset if you miss a day.

### Can I edit past entries?

Yes! Tap any entry in History to edit or delete it. Changes update your totals and goals automatically.

### Do goals expire?

Goals don't expire unless you set an end date. Monthly goals reset each month, yearly goals reset each year.

### Can I change a goal after creating it?

Currently, goals cannot be edited after creation. Delete and recreate the goal if needed. Future versions may include goal editing.

### How do I share my achievements?

When you achieve a goal or earn a badge, tap the "Share" button to:
- Share to social media
- Save to Photos
- Send via Messages or Email
- Share via AirDrop

### What's the difference between templates and categories?

- **Categories**: Types of savings (Skipped Purchase, Used Coupon, etc.)
- **Templates**: Saved entry configurations with amount, category, and note pre-filled

Templates are for quick entry of common savings, while categories help organize and analyze your data.
